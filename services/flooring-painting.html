Our Flooring & Painting Services

Flooring Installation

We offer expert installation of a wide range of flooring options to suit your style, budget, and lifestyle needs:

  • Hardwood Flooring: Solid hardwood installation with various species, finishes, and installation patterns.
  • Engineered Wood: A versatile alternative to solid hardwood that offers greater stability in areas with moisture concerns.
  • Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP): Waterproof, durable flooring that mimics the look of hardwood or stone at a more affordable price point.
  • Tile Flooring: Ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone tile installation for kitchens, bathrooms, and other areas.
  • Carpet Installation: Various carpet styles, textures, and padding options for comfortable, warm spaces.
  • Laminate Flooring: Affordable, durable flooring that resembles hardwood with easy maintenance.

Our flooring process includes removal of existing flooring, subfloor preparation, professional installation, and finishing. We help you select the right flooring material for each space in your home, considering factors like durability, maintenance, and aesthetic appeal.

Professional Painting

Our comprehensive painting services include both interior and exterior applications:

  • Interior Painting: Walls, ceilings, trim, doors, cabinets, and more with precise cutting-in and even coverage.
  • Exterior Painting: House exteriors, trim, doors, decks, and other outdoor surfaces with weather-resistant paints.
  • Cabinet Painting: Transform your kitchen or bathroom with professionally painted cabinets.
  • Color Consultation: Expert advice on color selection to achieve your desired look and feel.
  • Specialty Finishes: Faux finishes, accent walls, and decorative techniques for unique spaces.
  • Wallpaper Removal: Professional removal of old wallpaper and wall preparation for painting.

Our painting process includes thorough preparation (cleaning, sanding, priming), careful masking and protection of surfaces, application of premium paints, and detailed clean-up. We use low-VOC paints when requested and ensure proper ventilation during the painting process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to install new flooring?

The timeline for flooring installation depends on the type of flooring, the size of the area, and the condition of the subfloor. As a general guideline: Hardwood installation typically takes 1-3 days for an average-sized room, tile installation takes 2-4 days including drying time, and carpet or vinyl installation usually takes 1-2 days. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

What type of flooring is best for kitchens and bathrooms?

For kitchens and bathrooms, we typically recommend water-resistant or waterproof options such as porcelain or ceramic tile, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), or engineered hardwood. These materials stand up well to moisture and are easy to clean. Natural stone is also an excellent option for bathrooms but requires proper sealing. We'll help you select the best option based on your specific needs, budget, and aesthetic preferences.

How many coats of paint do you apply?

We typically apply two coats of paint after proper priming for most interior and exterior surfaces. This ensures even coverage, true color representation, and maximum durability. For darker colors or significant color changes, additional coats may be necessary. Our goal is to achieve a flawless finish that will look beautiful for years to come.

Do I need to move my furniture before you start work?

For flooring installation, the room needs to be completely clear of furniture. For painting, we can move smaller furniture items to the center of the room and cover them, but larger pieces should be removed if possible. We can discuss furniture moving options during your consultation. For an additional fee, we can arrange for furniture moving services to make the process easier for you.

How do I maintain my new flooring?

Each flooring type has specific maintenance requirements. For hardwood, regular sweeping and occasional damp mopping with a hardwood-specific cleaner is recommended. Tile floors can be cleaned with mild detergent and water. Luxury vinyl and laminate should be swept regularly and cleaned with manufacturer-recommended products. We provide detailed care instructions for your specific flooring upon completion of installation to help you maintain its beauty and extend its lifespan.

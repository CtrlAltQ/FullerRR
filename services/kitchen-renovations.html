Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical kitchen renovation take?

The timeline for a kitchen renovation depends on the scope of the project. A minor update might take 2-3 weeks, while a complete remodel typically takes 6-8 weeks. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

What is the average cost of a kitchen renovation?

Kitchen renovation costs vary widely based on the size of your kitchen, the quality of materials, and the extent of the changes. Minor updates might start around $15,000, while full custom renovations can range from $30,000 to $80,000+. We provide detailed, transparent pricing during our proposal phase.

Do I need to move out during a kitchen renovation?

Most clients remain in their homes during kitchen renovations. While there will be some disruption, we take steps to minimize the impact on your daily life. We can discuss setting up a temporary kitchen space and will always clean up at the end of each workday.

Do you handle all aspects of the renovation, including plumbing and electrical?

Yes, we provide comprehensive kitchen renovation services, including plumbing, electrical, carpentry, and finishing work. All of our subcontractors are licensed professionals who meet our high standards for quality and reliability.

Do you offer financing options for kitchen renovations?

We can provide information about financing options through our trusted partners. Many clients choose to finance their renovations through home equity loans or lines of credit, which often offer favorable terms for home improvement projects.

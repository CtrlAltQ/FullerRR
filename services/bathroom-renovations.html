Bathroom Renovation Services

Your bathroom should be more than just functional—it should be a personal retreat where you can relax and rejuvenate. At Fuller Restoration & Renovation, we specialize in creating bathrooms that combine beauty, comfort, and practicality.

Our comprehensive bathroom renovation services range from simple fixture updates to complete transformations. Whether you're looking to refresh your space with new tile and fixtures or completely redesign your bathroom with a custom shower, soaking tub, and premium vanities, our experienced team will bring your vision to life.

We understand that bathrooms present unique challenges, from plumbing and electrical considerations to moisture control and ventilation. Our expertise in these areas ensures that your renovation not only looks beautiful but also functions flawlessly and stands the test of time.

With over 15 years of experience renovating bathrooms across Middle Tennessee, we have the knowledge and skills to handle projects of any size and complexity. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine while delivering exceptional results that exceed your expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

The timeline for a bathroom renovation depends on the scope of the project. A simple update might take 1-2 weeks, while a complete remodel typically takes 3-5 weeks. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

What is the average cost of a bathroom renovation?

Bathroom renovation costs vary based on the size of your bathroom, the quality of materials, and the extent of the changes. Minor updates might start around $8,000, while full custom renovations can range from $20,000 to $50,000+. We provide detailed, transparent pricing during our proposal phase.

Can you help with design ideas for my bathroom?

Absolutely! Our team can provide design guidance based on your preferences, budget, and the latest trends. We'll help you select fixtures, tile, colors, and layouts that work together to create a cohesive and beautiful bathroom.

Do I need permits for a bathroom renovation?

Permits are typically required for bathroom renovations that involve changes to plumbing, electrical, or structural elements. As part of our service, we handle all necessary permits and ensure that all work meets or exceeds local building codes.

What should I consider when planning a bathroom renovation?

Key considerations include your budget, timeline, how long you plan to stay in your home, your daily routine, storage needs, lighting preferences, and any accessibility requirements. During our consultation, we'll discuss all these factors to ensure your renovation meets your specific needs.

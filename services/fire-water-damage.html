Fire & Water Damage Restoration Services

When disaster strikes your home, Fuller Restoration & Renovation is here to help you recover. Our comprehensive fire and water damage restoration services are designed to quickly assess the damage, mitigate further issues, and restore your home to its pre-damage condition or better.

Water damage from flooding, burst pipes, or leaking appliances can cause significant structural issues and lead to dangerous mold growth if not addressed promptly. Our team uses professional-grade equipment and proven techniques to extract water, thoroughly dry affected areas, and restore damaged materials.

Fire damage often extends beyond what's visible, with smoke and soot affecting areas far from the fire source. Our restoration process includes thorough cleaning of soot and smoke residue, odor removal, and structural repairs to ensure your home is safe and beautiful again.

We understand that experiencing fire or water damage is stressful and disruptive. That's why we work efficiently to minimize the impact on your daily life while delivering exceptional results. Our team coordinates with insurance companies to streamline the claims process, making your recovery as smooth as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We offer 24/7 emergency response services and typically arrive on-site within 1-2 hours of your call, depending on your location in Middle Tennessee. Quick response is crucial to minimize damage and begin the restoration process as soon as possible.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we work directly with all major insurance companies to streamline the claims process. We document all damage thoroughly, provide detailed estimates, and communicate with your insurance adjuster to ensure you receive the coverage you're entitled to. Our goal is to make the restoration process as stress-free as possible during this difficult time.

How long does the restoration process take?

The timeline for restoration varies depending on the extent of the damage. Minor water damage might be resolved in 3-5 days, while significant fire or flood damage could take several weeks to months, especially if structural repairs are needed. During your initial assessment, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your situation.

Can you save my belongings after water or fire damage?

Many belongings can be restored after water or fire damage using specialized cleaning techniques. We assess each item individually and use appropriate methods for restoration. For items that cannot be restored on-site, we can recommend professional content restoration services. We document all salvageable and non-salvageable items for insurance purposes.

What should I do immediately after experiencing water or fire damage?

For water damage: If safe, turn off the water source and electricity to affected areas. Remove small valuables from the area and mop up excess water if possible. For fire damage: Do not re-enter the property until fire officials declare it safe. In both cases, contact your insurance company and then call us immediately for emergency response. Do not attempt to clean soot, smoke, or contaminated water yourself, as this requires professional equipment and techniques.
